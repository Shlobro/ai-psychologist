# Arc

## Purpose
Every life has a developmental arc — a direction of movement from who a person was to who they are becoming. Understanding the arc is different from simply charting history: it asks what direction this person's life is pointing in, what they are moving away from, and what they are moving toward. The arc also captures trajectory and momentum: is this person growing, contracting, circling, or stuck? This file attempts to synthesize everything known into a sense of the long shape of this person's story.

## What to capture here
- The broad developmental movement across the full span of their life so far
- What they were like five or ten years ago versus now — the direction of change
- The core themes that have threaded through every chapter of their life
- What they seem to be moving toward — consciously and unconsciously
- What they seem to be moving away from
- Whether they are in a period of expansion, consolidation, contraction, or crisis
- The version of themselves they are working toward becoming
- The version of themselves they are trying to leave behind
- Patterns that have remained unchanged despite their efforts — areas of stuckness
- Areas of genuine growth and transformation over time
- Unfinished business from earlier chapters that is still demanding resolution
- The next chapter of their life as it seems to be forming
- What they would need to fully become the person they are trying to be
