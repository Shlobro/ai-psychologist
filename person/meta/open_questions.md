# Open Questions

## Purpose
No profile is ever complete. This file exists to hold what remains uncertain, unresolved, or still unasked — the questions that would most deepen understanding if answered, the gaps that remain in the picture, the threads that have been noticed but not yet followed. Maintaining active awareness of what you do not know about a person is as important as documenting what you do. Open questions also provide direction: they are the agenda for continued inquiry.

## What to capture here
- Questions about their history that remain unclear or unanswered
- Psychological patterns whose origins or meaning are not yet understood
- Contradictions that have been observed but not yet explained
- Behaviors or choices that do not yet make sense given what is known
- Important relationships or periods that have not yet been explored adequately
- Aspects of their inner world they have not disclosed or that remain opaque
- Questions you would most want to ask them if you had one honest conversation
- Things about them that feel important but cannot yet be named
- Areas where the data seems inconsistent and the reason is unclear
- Questions they themselves seem to be sitting with — their own unresolved dilemmas
- What would most change the profile if it turned out to be different from current assumptions
- What remains unknown about their future — the genuinely open possibilities
