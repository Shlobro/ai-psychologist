# Likes

## Purpose
What a person genuinely enjoys — not what they feel they should enjoy, but what actually produces pleasure, engagement, or delight — is a direct window into their character. Likes are not trivial. They reveal sensibility, values, and the texture of inner life. Authentic enjoyment is one of the most honest forms of self-expression available. This file documents the full range of what this person genuinely gravitates toward and finds pleasurable.

## What to capture here
- Activities and hobbies that produce genuine enjoyment
- Social situations they thrive in
- Environments that feel comfortable and enlivening
- Types of conversation they find genuinely engaging
- Things in nature or the physical world that move or delight them
- Sensory pleasures: smells, textures, sounds, tastes, sights that they love
- Intellectual topics or questions they find endlessly interesting
- Cultural experiences they seek out: music, art, films, books, events
- Small, everyday things that reliably improve their mood
- Things they loved as a child that they still love
- Things they have recently discovered they love
- What they would do if they had a completely free day with no obligations
- The things they like that surprise people who know them
